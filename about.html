About me

Front-end developer from Berlin, Germany

My name is Diana, but you can also call me Dee (A nickname which I've got from the military). I'm an Economics graduate originally from Israel, now based in Berlin, Germany. I'm an only child, yet my friends are considered no less than family. I currently work as an IT Administrator at a music start-up, which leads me to one of my biggest passions in life - music. I've been playing guitar since I was 13 y.o., harmonica too, and discovered I could sing at the age of 16. My taste in music is widely broad. My favourite genres are mainly classical, glam & hard rock, indie, power & thrash metal, R&B, american country and some 90s pop.

Apart from enjoying music I'm working on my 2nd Dan in Shotokan-Karate. This martial art type encompasses many element such as: kicking techniques, Kata as a fighting art, breathing correctly and even meditation. But amongst all that, to me it means pure love.
